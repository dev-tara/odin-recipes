Easy Homemade Ramen Recipe

A tastier and healthier instant ramen noodles recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoon grated garlic
  • 500ml broth (chicken or vegetable)
  • 500ml water
  • 15g dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 1 package of instant ramen (noodles only!!)
  • 20g chopped scallions or chives
  • 15g chopped kale (or spinach)
  • 60g shredded carrots
  • soy sauce
  • Siracha
  • Chilli oil
  • Soft boiled egg
  • Nori (Dried Seaweed)

Steps:

  1. Heat the sesame oil in a large skillet over medium low heat. Add the garlic and ginger; stir fry for 2 minutes or until soft and fragrant.
  2. Add the broth and the water. Bring to a simmer; add the mushrooms and simmer for 10 minutes or until the mushrooms have softened and the broth is flavorful.
  3. Add the instant noodles to the hot liquid and simmer for an additional 5 minutes or until the noodles have softened. Add the scallions and stir to combine.
  4. Remove from heat, stir in the kale and carrots, and top with dried seaweed and a soft-boiled egg (optional). Season with chili oil, hot sauce, sesame oil, and/or soy sauce and salt to taste.
